University of Manitoba Robotics Team

About us

UMRT - The University of Manitoba Robotics Team is an ambitious group of students interested in competing in the Canadian International Rover Challenge 2024! Our disciplines range from Computer Vision, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Embedded Systems, Wireless Communications, and Pedology. We have alot of plans for the future along with other types of events such as workshops, open comeptitions, and more!

Feel free to contact us at exec.umrt@gmail.com for any questions or inquiries.
